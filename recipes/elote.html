Elote - Mexican Street Corn

Description

This Elote, AKA Mexican Street Corn, recipe is the perfect side dish. It's the perfect combination of sweet, savory and spicy all at once. It is the perfect side dish to go with any Mexican-inspired meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups corn
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, minced
  • 2/3 cup cotija cheese
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Brown corn with butter. Add corn and butter to a skillet. Roast until slightly browned.
  2. Add primary ingredients and stir. Once the corn is cooked, add in lime juice, spices, mayonnaise and cheese. Stir until well combined.
  3. Top. Top with chopped cilantro and lime wedges.
